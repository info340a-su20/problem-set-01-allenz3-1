SHREK

Shrek is the main character of his eponymous movie series. In the first movie, he and his fairytale friends go to Lord Farquaad's castle to rescue Princess Fiona, who is locked in a castle tower. Fiona has a curse that turns her into an ogre every night, and is set to marry Lord Farquaad. Shrek and Fiona fall in love, run away, and Shrek turns Fiona into an ogre permanently, lifting her curse.

Why Shrek is awesome!

  1. His personality has many layers, like an onion.
  2. He doesn't allow trespassing in his swamp.
  3. He reminds you that it's not ogre now, and it isn't ogre until he says it's ogre.
